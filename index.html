Product Introduction

维骨康修复膜 (Ruigu Bio-membrane)

Ruigu Bio-membrane

维骨康修复膜是山东瑞骨生物科技有限公司研发的创新型生物材料，核心成分为生物相容性大分子、金属离子及天然植物多酚，兼具促进骨生成、血管新生与免疫调节多重功效。其低免疫原性与高生物相容性，确保其在外科手术中安全适用。

产品采用亲水微观结构，湿润状态下可紧密贴合骨壁，形成稳定屏障，为骨生长提供充足时间与免疫微环境。适用于口腔种植引导骨再生术、牙槽嵴增高/扩增术、骨缺损填充等场景，亦能用于骨质疏松患者的骨裂修复。

使用时需遵循无菌操作，可根据缺损范围剪裁膜片（超出边缘至少2mm），粗糙面朝向骨组织，光滑面贴附软组织。通过固定与减张缝合确保位置稳定，其高抗拉强度与亲水性助力血凝块形成与伤口闭合。产品规格涵盖直径16mm、35mm、90mm，为骨修复领域提供高效便捷的解决方案。

Ruigu Bio-membrane is an innovative biomaterial developed by Shandong Ruigu Biotechnology Co., Ltd. Its core components include biocompatible macromolecules, metal ions, and natural plant polyphenols, offering multiple benefits including bone formation promotion, angiogenesis, and immune regulation. With low immunogenicity and high biocompatibility, it ensures safe application in surgical procedures.

The product features a hydrophilic microscopic structure that closely adheres to bone walls when moistened, forming a stable barrier and providing sufficient time and immune microenvironment for bone growth. It is suitable for guided bone regeneration in dental implants, alveolar ridge augmentation/expansion, bone defect filling, and bone fracture repair in osteoporosis patients.

Usage requires sterile procedures, with the membrane cut according to the defect area (extending at least 2mm beyond the edges). The rough surface faces bone tissue while the smooth surface attaches to soft tissue. Position stability is ensured through fixation and tension-free suturing, while its high tensile strength and hydrophilicity aid blood clot formation and wound closure. Available in 16mm, 35mm, and 90mm diameters, it provides an efficient solution for bone repair.

About Us

山东瑞骨生物科技有限公司是一家专注于生物材料研发和生产的高科技企业。2024年，公司创始人魏绍银成功研制出天然可吸收生物膜Ruigu Bio-membrane。由于Ruigu Bio-membrane具有良好的骨再生和促血管生成效果，因此大大地简化了手术操作方法，进一步推动了骨再生领域的进步和发展。

我们致力于通过创新生物材料解决骨缺损修复的临床难题，为患者提供更安全、更有效的医疗解决方案。公司汇聚了一批在生物材料、医学和工程领域拥有丰富经验的专业人才，不断推进技术创新和产品研发。

Shandong Ruigu Biotechnology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise focusing on the research and development and production of biomaterials. In 2024, company founder Wei Shaoyin successfully developed the natural absorbable biomembrane, Ruigu Bio-membrane. Due to Ruigu Bio-membrane's excellent bone regeneration and angiogenesis effects, it greatly simplifies surgical procedures, further advancing progress and development in the field of bone regeneration.

We are committed to solving clinical challenges in bone defect repair through innovative biomaterials, providing patients with safer and more effective medical solutions. The company has gathered a team of professionals with rich experience in biomaterials, medicine, and engineering, continuously advancing technological innovation and product development.
